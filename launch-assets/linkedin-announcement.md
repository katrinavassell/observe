## Post

You know what your AI calls cost.
You don't know which features are profitable.

Every team I talk to has the same blind spot. You can see the inference bill. You can't see which features are underwater, which customers cost more to serve than they pay, or where your margin actually goes.

Stopping at cost-per-call is stopping halfway.

So we built Observe — free AI margin analytics that ties cost to revenue to margin:

— Install in under a minute. Copy the prompt, paste it into Cursor or Claude Code, Stripe auto-connects.

— Margin by feature, customer, and model. Red-flag the ones underwater before they sink the quarter.

— Trace cost across multi-step agent runs. Every span, every dollar, attributed to the feature that caused it.

— Simulate pricing changes against real customer data. See revenue impact and churn risk before you roll anything out.

— Alerts for cost spikes and margin floors.

Observe is free forever. When you're ready to act on what it surfaces — metering, entitlements, pricing experimentation — the full Tanso platform is one upgrade.

## Posting notes

- Post **Tuesday or Wednesday, 8–10am ET** — peaks LinkedIn tech engagement.
- **Don't edit after posting.** LinkedIn suppresses reach on edited posts.
- **First comment is yours.** Drop a follow-up: *"If you want the one-line install snippet, reply and I'll DM it."* That drives comment volume, which the algorithm rewards.
- **Don't @-tag the company page** in the post body — it caps reach. Put it in a comment instead.
- **Reply to every comment in the first hour.** Reach dies after 60 min if engagement stalls.
